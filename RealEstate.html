Dream House


Architecture
The design for this client’s “dream house on the water” capitalizes on every opportunity to incorporate views of the Long Island Sound within the home. The placement and expanse of the fir windows and sliding doors underscore the water’s proximity.



The design challenge

A design challenge consisted of refining the existing exterior and improving the complex layout of the interior in addition to enriching each space with fine craftsmanship. The footprint of the building would need to be reused, while the foundation walls would need to be raised to meet flood regulations. Innovative materials were used to produce an energy efficient thermal envelope and contemporary yet contextually appropriate facades.
